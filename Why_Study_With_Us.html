Friendly Environment

Masaryk University, Faculty of Arts, and the Dutch Department offer a welcoming and friendly study atmosphere. With a supportive community of students and faculty, the department fosters an engaging environment where you can thrive academically while exploring the Dutch language, literature, and culture.

Exchange Programs and Summer Schools

We offer a variety of exchange programs, including Erasmus+ and CEEPUS. Additionally, we organize regular summer schools focused on translation to and from Dutch, providing valuable international experience and practical skills.

Tradition and Quality

At our institution, tradition and quality go hand in hand. With a history of academic excellence and a commitment since 1919 to continuous growth, we are proud to be ranked 450th in the world by QS World University Rankings. This prestigious ranking reflects our strong academic foundations, innovative research, and dedication to providing students with a top-tier education.
