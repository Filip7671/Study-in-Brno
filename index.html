WHO WE ARE

Welcome to the Department of Dutch Language, Literature, and Culture at the Faculty of Arts, Masaryk University in Brno, Czechia!

Here, you’ll explore the rich world of Dutch language, literature, and culture. Our department offers the opportunity to master the language, study Dutch literature, and delve into the cultural traditions of the Netherlands and Belgium. Join us and become part of a dynamic community committed to the exploration of the Dutch-speaking world!

OUR TEACHERS

Mgr. Marta Kostelecká, Ph.D.

Marta Kostelecká, Assistant Professor in Dutch Studies, specializes in translation studies and contemporary Dutch literature, focusing on its reception in the Czech Republic. Additionally, she offers Dutch language courses for non-major students and the general public as part of lifelong learning. She aims to provide students with practical skills applicable in both their professional and personal lives.

Mgr. Sofie Rose-Anne W. Royeaerd, M.A.

Sofie Royeaerd is a lecturer in Dutch studies. Originally from Ghent, Belgium, she studied Dutch language and literature before pursuing further education in Amsterdam, where she developed an interest in language variation. In her language exercises, she uses interactive tasks and authentic materials to encourage reflection and discussion. In courses on Dutch and Belgian literature and culture, she provides an overview of their literary, cultural, and social development, offering essential context for understanding today’s Dutch-speaking regions.
